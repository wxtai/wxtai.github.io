About me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Software Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), supervised by Prof. Ting Zhong and Prof. Fan Zhou. Before that, I received the BS degree in Communication Engineering and MS degree in Software Engineering at UESTC in 2019 and 2022, respectively.

The long-term goal of my research is to build a machine that can work well on limited, complex and uncertain data emerging in real-world applications. To this end, my research focuses on controllable generative probabilistic models and trustworthy neural networks.
